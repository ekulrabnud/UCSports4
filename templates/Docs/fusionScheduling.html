Crestron LL Suite TV and Club Bar TV Scheduling procedure

  1. Go to http://crestap01/fusion/webclient/monitoring/
  2. On left hand side Click ‘+’ on United Center folder
  3. Click ‘+’ on LLSUITES” folder then select LL01
  4. Click Schedule TAB in right-hand window
  5. Click Meeting. Prompt opens “What will this meeting be?”. Select Action as type of meeting.
  6. Give meeting name and set START/END times.
  7. Select Actions tab.
  8. Click “Meeting Start” then check boxes on left column for “LL SUITE TV [EVENT ON]” and “CLUB TV [ON]” and then click “Add Action” (top left) to add these actions to the meeting start.
  9. Repeat same procedure for “Meeting End” but this time select check boxes for “LL SUITE OFF” and “CLUB TV [OFF]”. Then “Add Action”.
  10. Select “Rooms” tab
  11. Click ‘+’ on “United Center” folder then click ‘+’ on LL Suites folder then check box next to “LL Suites”. This will select all LL Suites. Scroll to bottom and then select checkbox next to “Club Level”. Click “Add” (top left)
  12. Check previous settings then click “Save and Close”
